NeoMatrix Frame Creator

Welcome to the NeoMatrix Frame Creator! Use this tool to design LED matrix frames for your animations. Simply click on any cell in the grid to toggle it on/off.

  • Orientation: Switch between different origin corners.
  • Grid Size: Adjust the grid dimensions.

This was originally designed for the final project in the University of Florida class CEN4907C, Computer Engineering Design 1, on the WS2812 LED matrix.
